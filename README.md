# Cookie Clicker Tkinter
A basic cookie clicker game made in Tkinter (Python library).

## Tutorial
Click the button labelled 'Click' to earn cookies.

### Rebirth Upgrade
A rebirth upgrade will double your cookies per click, however it will reset your cookies to 0, use it wisely.

### Passive Upgrade
A passive upgrade will double your passive cookie generation (cookies also generate when you aren't clicking), it costs 10 cookies, but it is a good investment.
